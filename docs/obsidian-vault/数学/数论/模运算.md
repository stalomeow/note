# 模运算

$$
a \bmod b = a - b \left\lfloor \frac{a}{b} \right\rfloor
$$


## 性质 1

$$
(a \pm b) \bmod p = ((a \bmod p) \pm (b \bmod p)) \bmod p
$$


## 性质 2


$$
ab \bmod p = ((a \bmod p)(b \bmod p)) \bmod p
$$


## 性质 3

$$
a^b \bmod p = (a \bmod p)^b \bmod p
$$
